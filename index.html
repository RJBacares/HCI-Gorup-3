Results

This section presents the findings from usability testing, highlighting key issues identified during evaluation. It discusses what the results mean in relation to the prototype’s design and usability. It then provides recommendations for improvements and suggests specific design enhancements, along with an attached PDF of the completed heuristic evaluation workbook.
